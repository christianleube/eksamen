Social media kursus

The student who attended the Social Media course gets the following badge. When finishing this course, the student has been introduced to the essential skills, so the student is able to use Facebook professionally – on a practical level.

Earnings Criteria
The student has been introduced to Facebook ads and received knowledge about how to analyze ads on a professional level. Furthermore, the student has learned how to create audiences and received an insight into the audiences. The student was likewise introduced to the difference between Business Manager vs. Personal Ad Account. The student has obtained knowledge of how to use Facebook for not only personal use but also for marketing purposes.
